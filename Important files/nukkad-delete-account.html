NUKKAD Account & Data Deletion Request

NUKKAD allows users to request deletion of their account and associated personal data at any time.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the NUKKAD app.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Select Delete Account.
  4. Confirm the deletion request.

If you are unable to access the app, you can request account deletion by contacting us via email.

Email: vedaeetechnologies@gmail.com

Please include your registered phone number in your request for verification purposes.

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • Phone number
  • Profile information
  • Messages and chat history
  • Call history
  • Device tokens and related identifiers

Data Retention Policy

All user data is permanently deleted within 30 days of receiving the deletion request, unless retention is required by law.
